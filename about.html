Our Mission

Helping Businesses Grow Better

Founded in 2018, HubFlow was born from a simple idea: businesses shouldn't need a dozen disconnected tools to manage their growth. We set out to build an all-in-one platform that brings marketing, sales, service, and operations together in one seamless experience.

Today, over 10,000 companies in 120+ countries rely on HubFlow to attract new customers, close more deals, and deliver exceptional service. Our mission is to make powerful business tools accessible to companies of all sizes â€” from startups to enterprises.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at HubFlow.

Customer First

Every decision we make starts with the customer. Their success is our success, and we're committed to delivering exceptional value at every touchpoint.

Innovation

We constantly push boundaries to build better tools. Our R&D team invests heavily in AI and machine learning to stay ahead of the curve.

Trust & Security

We take data protection seriously. SOC 2 certified with enterprise-grade security, so your business data is always safe and compliant.

Collaboration

Great things happen when teams work together. We build tools that break down silos and foster collaboration across departments.

Our Journey

Key milestones that shaped HubFlow into what it is today.

2018
Founded in San Francisco

Alex and Sarah founded HubFlow with $2M in seed funding, launching with a basic CRM and marketing automation tool.

2019
1,000 Customers Milestone

Reached 1,000 paying customers. Launched Sales Hub and raised $15M Series A led by Accel Partners.

2021
Series B & Global Expansion

Raised $50M Series B. Opened offices in London and Singapore. Launched Service Hub and Operations Hub.

2023
AI-Powered Features

Introduced AI-powered analytics, predictive lead scoring, and automated content generation across all hubs.

2025
10,000+ Customers Worldwide

Surpassed 10,000 customers in 120+ countries. Named a Leader in Gartner's Magic Quadrant for CRM. Team grew to 500+ employees.
